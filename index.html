About Us

Dusty Trail Dog Walking and Pet Sitting is Central New Jersey’s professional pet sitting company, servicing parts of Union and Middlesex Counties. We offer the highest quality pets care to assure families total peace of mind while they’re away.

By getting to know you and your pet, we customize our services to meet your needs. Our dedicated staff send you up-to-date reports via text and email to let you know exactly how your pets and home are doing. You never have to wonder what they are up to. We’ll give you the full scoop. We perform visits 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Whether you’re at work, on vacation, or out to dinner Dusty Trail has it covered. Every measure is taken to ensure your confidence in our service, because a pet owner at ease makes for happy pets… and “happy pets” is our business.
